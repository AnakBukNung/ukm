ABOUT US

The Student Activity Unit (UKM) is an organization that provides a platform for students to explore, develop, and enhance their interests and talents across a wide range of fields. These areas include arts, sports, social activities, leadership, and academic development. Through various programs, workshops, competitions, and community-based initiatives, UKM helps students gain valuable experience, build new skills, and foster meaningful connections with others who share similar passions. It serves as an important space for personal growth, creativity, and holistic development throughout a student’s university journey.
